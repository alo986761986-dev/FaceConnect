# Google Play Store Listing Guide for FaceConnect

## App Information

### App Name
**FaceConnect - Biometric Social Network**

### Short Description (80 characters max)
Connect through facial recognition. Social networking reimagined with AI.

### Full Description (4000 characters max)
FaceConnect is a revolutionary social networking app that uses cutting-edge facial recognition technology to help you connect with friends and discover new connections.

**Key Features:**

🔹 **Facial Recognition**
- Scan faces in photos to identify and tag friends
- Compare two photos to find matching faces
- Secure biometric authentication options

🔹 **Social Feed**
- Share posts with photos and videos
- Like, comment, and react to content
- Highlight your favorite posts

🔹 **Reels**
- Watch and create short-form videos
- Vertical scrolling TikTok-style feed
- Like, comment, and share reels

🔹 **Live Streaming**
- Go live with real-time video streaming
- WebRTC-powered low-latency streams
- Chat with viewers and receive reactions
- AI visual effects and filters

🔹 **Messaging**
- Private one-on-one conversations
- Group chats with admin controls
- HD video and voice calls
- Screen sharing during calls

🔹 **Privacy First**
- Control who sees your content
- Export your data anytime (GDPR compliant)
- Secure authentication options

🔹 **Modern Design**
- Stunning Neo-Noir dark theme
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Optimized for mobile devices

Download FaceConnect today and experience the future of social networking!

## Graphics Assets Needed

### App Icon
- Size: 512 x 512 px
- Format: 32-bit PNG (with alpha)
- Already created: /app/frontend/public/icons/icon-512x512.png

### Feature Graphic
- Size: 1024 x 500 px
- Format: JPG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- Content: FaceConnect logo with tagline "Biometric Social Network"
- Background: Gradient from #00E5FF to #7C3AED

1. Login/Auth page (shows branding)

4. Chat/Messaging screen

6. Live streaming
7. Profile page
8. Settings page

Screenshot sizes:
- Phone: 16:9 ratio (1920x1080 or 1080x1920)

### Video (Optional but recommended)
- Length: 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- Format: MP4, WebM, or OGG
- Content: App walkthrough showing key features

## Build Instructions

Follow the ANDROID_BUILD.md guide to create your release APK/AAB:

1. Install Capacitor CLI
2. Run: npx cap sync android
3. Open in Android Studio
4. Generate signed APK/AAB
5. Upload to Google Play Console

## Recommended Release Strategy

### Alpha/Beta Testing
1. Create a closed testing track
2. Invite 10-20 testers
3. Gather feedback for 1-2 weeks
4. Fix critical issues

### Production Release
1. Start with limited rollout (10-25%)
2. Monitor crash reports and reviews
3. Gradually increase to 100%
